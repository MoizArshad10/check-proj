About Us

GALLERIA - PAINT YOUR DREAMS


Galleria is the passion for your Art. Art is synonymous with passion because art is the most beautiful way to express your deepest emotions onto a blank canvas. Simply put, art is passion in color.


Galleria came into being when three students who happened to be aesthetes came together and realised there is endless talent and passion in Pakistan for art but little support for it. From here they embarked on a difficult but rewarding journey to create a platform that would bridge this gap and bring artists and art lovers.

Within just a few months of its existence, Galleria has now over 100 artists onboard from all across the country specializing in acrylics, Canvas, pastels, calligraphy, landscapes, pop art, digital illustrations, basically every form of art you can think of.

Galleria is Pakistan's first online platform that is built for art lovers and artists. Galleria is not just a buying and selling site for art but it is an initiative to promote art in Pakistan and make it easily accessible and available to the masses. Our vision is to create a space where local artists find support and encouragement to explore their creativity and talent, and showcase their art to appreciative individuals

Why Galleria for Artists?

By uploading your art pieces on our online art gallery you can reach a much wider audience and increase your chances of generating sales. Galleria not only gives you a platform to showcase your art but also markets it to increase your reach. We also have a dedicated customer service team that facilitates potential buyers of your art and makes the whole process of selling your art smooth and hassle-free. Not only this but Galleria also delivers the artwork on your behalf to the buyer, collects the payment and transfers it to you in no time. Our logistics team works day and night to ensure your artworks reaches its destinations all over Pakistan. We bear the entire burden from finding potential buyers for you to delivering your artwork to those buyers and transferring your payment to you so that you can just relax and focus on creating.

Why Galleria for Customers?

Galleria provides you with all kinds of artwork in one e-gallery. Now you do not have to run from store to store, artist to artist to find the perfect painting for your home or office. We have artists from all over Pakistan under our umbrella providing you with a variety of artwork from calligraphy, to paintings & phone cases and so SO much more. Anything you are looking for, we have surely got it at very reasonable prices. You will not find such affordable art of remarkable quality elsewhere. Simply select your favourite category and check out the art, add your desired piece to the cart, proceed to check out and voila! Your painting will be at your door in no time. It's that simple. Galleria is the only online art gallery in Pakistan providing you this much variety and housing hundreds of artists from all over the country.
